================================================================================
DIRECTIONAL LOGIC
================================================================================

SE (South-East):
  • Primary view: facing south-east toward camera-right
  • Shows front of character, full face visible
  • Source direction for most hand-drawn frames

SW (South-West):
  • Horizontal flip of SE
  • Facing south-west toward camera-left
  • Mirrors SE for left-facing movement

NE (North-East):
  • Back view, facing away from camera
  • Generated from flipped SE (shows cloak back)
  • Character moving to upper-right of screen

NW (North-West):
  • Back view, facing away from camera
  • Generated from flipped NE
  • Character moving to upper-left of screen

Light source maintained from top-left in all directions.
